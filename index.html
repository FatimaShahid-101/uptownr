About Us

Uptown Recruitments is a boutique HR firm offering you an unparalleled personalized service. We also provide value-added services as pre-screening of the candidates (at the required location), reference checks, assistance in visa processing & tie-up with agencies at preferred location to facilitate speedy execution. We focus on fulfilling the staffing requirement of corporates and companies, across boundaries. We specialize in prompt. completion of required mandates by providing candidates that are an exact match.

Our Vision

We believe that when our people thrive, so do our clients and our communities. Our objective is to empower individuals to inspire their colleagues, friends, families and communities through their work.

Our work process

Recruitment Queries

Uptown Recruits believes in human capital solutions that guarantee the most successful and expedient solution in any given industry and geography. We generate candidate flow to compliment any contingent search.

Contract and Visa Processing

Matching individual's previous experience with job description and expected role followed by telephonic interviews. The final selected candidate is provided with visa assistance and travel support.

Arrival of the candidate

Assisting through the visa and processing our team receives the candidate and after clearing medical fitness and orientation about environment the candidate continues with the contract.
